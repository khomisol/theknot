## Brainstorming Techniques

### 1. Classic Brainstorm
Free-flowing idea generation with no judgment.

**Rules:**
- No criticism during ideation
- Wild ideas welcome
- Build on others' ideas
- Go for quantity

### 2. SCAMPER
Systematic modification of existing ideas.

- **S**ubstitute - What can be replaced?
- **C**ombine - What can be merged?
- **A**dapt - What can be adjusted?
- **M**odify - What can be changed?
- **P**ut to other uses - New applications?
- **E**liminate - What can be removed?
- **R**everse - What if opposite?

### 3. Six Thinking Hats
Different perspectives on the same problem.

- ðŸŽ© **White** - Facts and data
- ðŸŽ© **Red** - Emotions and intuition
- ðŸŽ© **Black** - Caution and risks
- ðŸŽ© **Yellow** - Benefits and optimism
- ðŸŽ© **Green** - Creativity and alternatives
- ðŸŽ© **Blue** - Process and organization

### 4. Reverse Brainstorm
How could we make this WORSE?

Then flip each answer to find solutions.

### 5. Random Word Association
Use random stimulus to spark new connections.

### 6. Worst Possible Idea
Start with terrible ideas to remove fear of judgment.

## Session Structure

### 1. Setup (5 min)
- Define the challenge
- Set ground rules
- Create safe space

### 2. Warm-up (5 min)
- Quick creative exercise
- Get minds loose
- Build energy

### 3. Diverge (20 min)
- Generate many ideas
- No judgment
- Build and combine
- Capture everything

### 4. Converge (10 min)
- Group similar ideas
- Vote on favorites
- Identify themes

### 5. Refine (10 min)
- Develop top ideas
- Add detail
- Identify next steps
