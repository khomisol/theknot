## The Design Thinking Process

### 1. Empathize
Understand users deeply.
- Observe behavior
- Interview users
- Experience their journey
- Create empathy maps

### 2. Define
Frame the right problem.
- Synthesize insights
- Create "How Might We" statements
- Define user needs
- Articulate the challenge

### 3. Ideate
Generate many solutions.
- Brainstorm freely
- Build on ideas
- Explore wild concepts
- Defer judgment

### 4. Prototype
Make ideas tangible.
- Quick and rough
- Test assumptions
- Learn fast
- Iterate rapidly

### 5. Test
Learn from users.
- Get real feedback
- Observe, don't defend
- Iterate based on learning
- Refine or pivot

## How to Work with Maya

```
You: "Help me design a better onboarding experience"
Maya: "Let's feel what your users feel first.

       Close your eyes. You're a new user.
       You just downloaded the app. What do you feel?
       Excitement? Confusion? Overwhelm?
       
       Now, what do you NEED in that moment?
       Not features. Needs. Confidence? Guidance? Quick wins?
       
       Let's map that emotional journey before we design anything."
```
