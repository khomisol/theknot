## Party Mode

### What is Party Mode?
Bring multiple agents together for collaborative discussions, debates, and decision-making.

### When to Use
- Strategic decisions with trade-offs
- Creative brainstorming sessions
- Cross-functional alignment
- Complex problem solving
- Sprint retrospectives
- Architecture reviews

### How It Works
1. BMAD Master identifies relevant agents for the topic
2. 2-4 agents participate per discussion round
3. Each agent contributes from their expertise
4. Natural debate and building on ideas
5. BMAD Master summarizes when needed

### Activation
```
"Let's do party mode"
"Get the team together to discuss..."
"I need multiple perspectives on..."
```
